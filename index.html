Learn AI Governance with Phoebe

Govern the AI you build and deploy - before the regulator does

AI governance stopped being optional the day the EU AI Act became law. It reaches any company whose AI output is used in the EU - your Singapore firm included - and fines run to 7% of global turnover. A leader track teaches the Act's risk tiers, who is on the hook, and how NIST AI RMF and ISO/IEC 42001 turn the law into a runnable program. A practitioner track builds that program: classify a system, meet the high-risk obligations, and prove it. The running case is Vantage, a Singapore firm shipping an AI hiring tool into the EU.
